Our Priorities

Strengthening the local economy means supporting small businesses, attracting responsible development, and investing in infrastructure that creates jobs. Economic opportunity focuses on workforce readiness, smart growth, and ensuring residents can earn a living wage while local businesses thrive.

A strong community needs safe, stable, and affordable housing options. This priority emphasizes thoughtful planning, preservation of existing neighborhoods, and collaboration with partners to ensure housing meets the needs of families, seniors, and working residents- without sacrificing community character.

As the population ages, communities must adapt. Supporting aging residents includes access to healthcare, transportation, housing options, and community resources that promote independence, dignity, and quality of life for older adults and their caregivers.

Investing in youth is investing in the future. Youth opportunity focuses on access to education, enrichment programs, mentorship. recreation, and workforce pathways that help young people develop skills, confidence, and a sense of belonging.

Honoring veterans means more than recognition-it means ensuring access to services, housing support, employment opportunities, and community resources. This priority centers on helping veterans and their families successfully transition and remain supported long after their service ends.
